Neurodiversity in Tech

Neurodiversity in Tech (NDTech) is an internship program that trains neurodiverse adults on workplace practices, project management, and game development. Over 9 weeks, intern teams will create an educational game based on the requirements of an industry client.

For the NDTech 2025 internship, I was hired as a coach and oversaw two teams of 5 students. During the program, I taught the interns Unreal Engine game development and team communication strategies for efficient workflow. I took a "hands-on" approach by walking interns through complex programming tasks, drawing diagrams to illustrate algorithms, and creating assignments to test learning.

As a result, my teams created two games: Incident Commander, a fire fighter real-time strategy game, and Subterranean Wayfinding, an animal simulation game.
